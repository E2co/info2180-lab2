Cole Palmer is a talented young footballer who has quickly become one of the most promising players in the English Premier League. Born on May 6, 2002, in Manchester, England, Palmer began his youth career with Burnley FC before joining Manchster City's youth academy system.

Palmer was then bought by Chelsea Football Club in January 2022. He made his debut for Chelsea in February 2022 against Luton Town in the English Premier League where he scored his first Premier League goal.
